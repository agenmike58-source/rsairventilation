01

Restaurant hoods & exhaust systems

Our core specialty since founding in 1985. Ventilation R.S. Air is one of the few companies on the South Shore specializing in commercial kitchen hoods — a highly technical domain requiring mastery of fire codes, airflow design and commercial kitchen configurations.

Whether it's a wall-mounted hood, island hood, pizza oven or industrial rotisserie, we design, install and maintain systems tailored to your cooking volume and kitchen layout.

Wall-mounted, island and back-shelf hoods
Airflow sizing based on cooking type
Makeup air systems
Stainless steel and aluminium baffle filters
Automatic fire suppression (Ansul / Amerex)
Duct cleaning and degreasing
NFPA 96 & NBC compliance
Compliance reports for insurers and inspectors
Mandatory fire compliance. Building codes require commercial hood cleaning and maintenance between one and four times per year depending on cooking type. We issue the compliance certificates required by your insurers.

Rooftop units (RTU)

Rooftop air handling units are the standard solution for conditioning large commercial spaces. Restaurants, retail stores, warehouses and institutional buildings benefit from centralized, discreet and efficient air distribution.

Our team handles equipment selection, rooftop placement, connection to interior ductwork, commissioning and controller setup.

Supply and installation of RTUs (Carrier, Lennox, Daikin)
Replacement of aging units
Commissioning and programming
Connection to existing ductwork
Annual maintenance contracts
High-efficiency units available

04

Wall-mounted heat pumps

Wall-mounted heat pumps have become the preferred solution for homes, condos and small businesses that want to combine heating and cooling in one compact, quiet and highly efficient unit. With today's low-temperature models (-30°C), year-round heating in Quebec is fully achievable.

We install single-split and multi-split systems to serve multiple rooms or spaces from a single outdoor unit.

Single-split and multi-split heat pumps
Daikin, Mitsubishi, Carrier, Lennox brands
Cold-climate models rated to -30°C
Clean installation, no trenching required
Eligible for Hydro-Québec rebate programs
Commissioning and user training
Several provincial and Hydro-Québec rebate programs apply to qualifying heat pumps. Ask us for details when requesting your quote.
05

HRV air exchangers

A heat recovery ventilator (HRV) renews the indoor air in your home while recovering up to 80% of the heat from exhaust air. The result: healthy, fresh air without condensation or mould, at a fraction of the energy cost.

We handle the complete HRV installation including distribution network design, connection to your existing system and airflow balancing.

HRV and ERV (energy recovery ventilators)
Distribution duct network design
Integration with existing heating system
Airflow balancing
Filter cleaning and replacement (annual maintenance)
Venmar, Broan, Lifebreath brands
Popular with restaurant owners
